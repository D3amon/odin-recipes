Mac And Cheese

Description

The following will be the recipe for Mac and cheese and a list of ingredients.

you will be able to make your very own delicious mac and cheese at home with the following ingredients and steps.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • fine chopped onion
  • all purpose flour
  • milk
  • salt
  • dry mustard
  • ground black pepper
  • elbow macaroni
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • proccessed cheese (more commonly known as melting cheese)

Steps

  1. preheat oven (350farenheit or 175 celcius)
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Saut√© onion for 2 minutes. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Then stir in milk, salt, mustard, and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until mixture boils and thickens.
  4. Meanwhile, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  5. Add Cheddar and American cheeses to milk mixture; stir until cheese melts.
  6. Combine cheese sauce and macaroni in a 2-quart baking dish; mix well.
  7. Cook macaroni according to package directions.
  8. While macaroni is cooking, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth. Slowly pour in milk, stirring constantly until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Stir in cheese until melted.
  9. Drain macaroni and stir it into the cheese sauce.
  10. Pour into a 2-quart casserole dish.
  11. Bake in preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes before serving.
